ABOUT US

Pepe's Pizza Parlour opened 27 years ago and has been a Nairobi mainstay ever since, offering authentic Italian pizza unlike any other in the city.

With our large variety of quality pizza we thrive on the simple thrill of good food and drink.

Under the watchful eye of our Executive Chef,we faithfully incorpote our occasional signature Pepe twist in each pizza.

At Pepe's any meal is an occasion.
